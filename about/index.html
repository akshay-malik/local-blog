me

Akshay Malik - Currently working for City of Philadelphia's OTIS (Office of Transportation, Infrastructure & Sustainability). Focused on making urban infrastructure safer and smarter using data and design. Huge urban policy and data nerd. Studied and practiced architecture for several years. Studied city planning and now work for a city government. Love travelling, math and coding. Family guy.

blog

This blog is a compilation of my analysis and opnions on data science, public policy, urban mobility, architecture and system design. Within these broad categories, some topics/tags that I tend to think and write about include issues of equity, urban design, design theory, privacy and accessibility. I know it seems all over the place, but I believe that subjects are mankind's way to process information, not the way information exists itself. In this process of classification, we tend to loose a ton. I tend to learn from edge conditions and intersection or edge conditions of subjects provide me the most condusive opportunities to seek new ways to persieve the world.
